# The-Nine-Yang-Manual
The Inner Canon of Silicon Masters

这里不只是知识的堆砌，更是从 RTL 到 GDS、从架构到时序、从算法到硅后分析的系统修炼。
它记录的不只是代码与电路，更是每一次 Timing Closure 的鏖战、每一次 Bug Debug 的博弈、每一次 Tape-out 前夜的极限冲锋。

在这里：

- Verilog/SystemVerilog 是剑法
- 时钟与复位是经脉
- CDC/RDC 是心魔
- STA 是内功修为
- 功耗优化是轻功身法
- Debug 能力则是真正的“护体神功”

《九阳神功》的目标，不是培养只会“写代码”的工程师，而是打造真正理解芯片本质、掌控系统复杂度、敢于挑战极限PPA的芯片设计高手。

This is not merely a collection of notes.
It is a complete journey through silicon craftsmanship — from architecture to RTL, from timing closure to physical implementation, from algorithms to post-silicon debugging.

Every page captures the battles behind every successful tape-out:

- The elegance of microarchitecture
- The discipline of STA
- The hidden dangers of CDC/RDC
- The art of power optimization
- The obsession with PPA
- And the relentless pursuit of stable, high-performance silicon

Here, Verilog is the sword.
Clocks and resets are the meridians.
Debugging is the true inner force.

The Nine Yang Manual is built for engineers who seek more than coding skills —
for those who want to master complexity, understand the essence of hardware, and push silicon beyond its limits.
